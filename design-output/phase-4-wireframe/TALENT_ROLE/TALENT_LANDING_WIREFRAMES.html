P2: TALENT FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started

Is CastMatch really free to join?
Yes! Creating a basic profile is completely free. You can upgrade to premium plans for additional features and unlimited applications.
How do I know the casting calls are legitimate?
All casting directors and production houses are verified by our team. We check business registrations and past projects before approval.
Do I need professional photos to create a profile?
While professional photos help, you can start with good quality smartphone photos. Focus on clear, well-lit images that show your appearance accurately.
Can I apply for roles outside my city?
Absolutely! Many productions offer relocation support, and with our video audition feature, you can audition from anywhere.
How quickly will I start getting auditions?
This varies based on your profile completeness and the current casting calls. Most talents with complete profiles receive their first opportunity within 2 weeks.
