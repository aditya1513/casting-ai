CastMatch - Talent Calendar & Schedule Management Wireframes

Purpose: Comprehensive scheduling system for talents to manage auditions, callbacks, shootings, and availability settings with calendar integration.

Information Architecture

  • Route: /dashboard/calendar
  • User Type: Authenticated Talent
  • Priority: P1 - Critical Path
  • Dependencies: Calendar API, Google/Outlook Integration, Notification Service

Event Types

  • Auditions: Scheduled casting calls with location and requirements
  • Callbacks: Follow-up auditions after initial selection
  • Shootings: Confirmed bookings and production dates
  • Rehearsals: Practice sessions and workshops
  • Blocked Time: Personal unavailability periods

Key Features

  • Month/Week/Day/List view options
  • Drag-and-drop event management
  • Availability heat map for casting directors
  • Two-way calendar sync (Google, Outlook, Apple)
  • Automated reminders and notifications
  • Conflict detection and resolution
