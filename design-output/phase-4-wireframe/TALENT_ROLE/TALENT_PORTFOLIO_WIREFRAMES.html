CastMatch - Talent Portfolio Management Wireframes

Purpose: Complete media management system for talents to upload, organize, and showcase their portfolio materials including photos, videos, and audio reels.

Information Architecture

  • Route: /dashboard/portfolio
  • User Type: Authenticated Talent
  • Priority: P1 - Critical Path
  • Dependencies: Media Storage Service, CDN, Video Processing

Media Categories

  • Headshots: Professional photos for primary profile
  • Performance Photos: Action shots from productions
  • Video Reels: Acting showreels and performances
  • Audio Samples: Voice recordings and dialogue
  • Documents: Resume, certificates, references

Key Features

  • Drag-and-drop upload with progress tracking
  • Automatic image optimization and video compression
  • Media tagging and categorization
  • Portfolio builder for custom collections
  • Usage analytics and view tracking
  • Watermarking options for protected content
