General Questions

What is CastMatch and how does it work?
CastMatch is India's premier casting platform that connects talented artists with casting directors and production houses. Our AI-powered platform makes it easy to discover, audition, and hire the perfect talent for any project. Simply create a profile, showcase your work, and start connecting with opportunities that match your skills and experience.
Is CastMatch free to use?
CastMatch offers both free and premium plans. Basic profiles are free forever, allowing you to create a profile and apply for roles. Premium plans unlock advanced features like priority visibility, unlimited applications, and detailed analytics.
How do I get verified on CastMatch?
Verification helps build trust and credibility. To get verified, go to your profile settings, click on "Get Verified", and upload your government ID and professional credentials. Our team reviews applications within 24-48 hours.

For Talent

How do I make my profile stand out?
Complete your profile 100%, add high-quality photos and videos, keep your availability updated, respond quickly to messages, and get verified. Profiles with professional headshots and demo reels get 3x more views.
How many roles can I apply for?
Free users can apply for up to 10 roles per month. Premium members get unlimited applications plus priority visibility to casting directors.
What should I include in my portfolio?
Include professional headshots, full-body shots, performance reels, voice samples if applicable, previous work samples, and any awards or certifications. Keep files under 50MB each for optimal loading.

For Casting Directors

How do I post a casting call?
From your dashboard, click "New Project", fill in project details, add role requirements, set application deadline, and publish. You can manage applications, schedule auditions, and communicate with talent all from one place.
Can I search for talent without posting a role?
Yes! Use our advanced search to browse talent profiles anytime. Filter by location, age, experience, skills, and more. Save talents to shortlists for future projects.
How does the audition scheduling work?
Set available time slots, invite selected talents, and they can book their preferred slot. The system sends automatic reminders and you can conduct video auditions directly through the platform.
