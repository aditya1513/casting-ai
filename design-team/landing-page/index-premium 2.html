Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CastMatch

How does the AI-powered matching work? ⌄
Our AI analyzes your script and character requirements, then matches them against our talent database considering factors like acting experience, skills, physical attributes, and availability. The system learns from your preferences over time to provide increasingly accurate recommendations.
Is my data and script information secure? ⌄
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption to protect all data. Scripts and production information are stored securely with restricted access. We also offer NDAs and confidentiality agreements for all parties involved in the casting process.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime? ⌄
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing period.
How do video auditions work on the platform? ⌄
You can request video auditions from shortlisted talent directly through the platform. Actors receive your audition brief and can record and submit their auditions. You can review, comment, and share these videos with your team for collaborative decision-making.
What kind of support do you offer? ⌄
We offer email support for all plans, with priority support for Professional users (response within 4 hours). Enterprise customers get a dedicated account manager and phone support. We also have comprehensive documentation and video tutorials.
Can I integrate CastMatch with other production tools? ⌄
Professional and Enterprise plans include integrations with popular production management tools. Enterprise customers get API access for custom integrations. We support exports to common formats and can work with your existing workflow.
How verified are the talent profiles? ⌄
All talent profiles go through a verification process including identity verification, portfolio review, and reference checks. We maintain high standards to ensure you're connecting with genuine, professional actors.
Is there a mobile app available? ⌄
Yes, CastMatch is available as a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works seamlessly on all mobile devices. Native iOS and Android apps are coming soon for Professional and Enterprise users with offline capabilities.
Can actors apply to casting calls directly? ⌄
Yes, you can publish open casting calls that actors can apply to directly. You can set specific criteria to automatically filter applications. The AI also suggests relevant casting calls to actors based on their profiles.
What payment methods do you accept? ⌄
We accept all major credit and debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and international wire transfers for Enterprise accounts. All payments are processed securely through industry-standard payment gateways.
